Fact Hunter

General Rules

                       
 1 ) A team shall consist of max two students and only one team from each participating college can participate. 
 2) The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be  subjected to any change. 
 3) Participants shall not be allowed to use mobile electronic instruments. 
 4) First there will be an Elimination Round and after there will be Three Final Rounds to determine the winner of the Fact Hunter.

EliminationRound

               1) Each team would be given a set of question paper containing 20 multiple choice objective type questions.
               2) Time limit - 20 minutes.
               3) Only 6 Teams would be selected for final stage rounds.
               4) In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 5 questions would be asked for final selection.
               5) The selected teams shall have to appear for the final round.

Final Rounds(Round 1):

               1)Each team would be asked 1 question each. (Objective type or specific answer questions)
               2) 10 marks for each correct answer.
               3) If a team cannot answer the question, they can pass the question & then the question would be forwarded                             to the next team.
              4) Answering time is only 20 seconds.
              5) Team discussion is allowed.

Round 2 (Rapid Fire):

              1) Each team will be asked as many questions as possible one after another in one minute of time.
              2) 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer.
              3) If a team cannot answer the question, they can say pass for the next question. The question will not be                                  forwarded to the next team.

Round 3 (Visual round):

             1) Each Team would be asked 2 visual questions. ( 1 slide in each round )
             2) Answering time is only 20 seconds.
             3) 10 marks for each correct answer.
             4) No passing to the next team. Audience can answer.



Bug Challenge

General Rules

                         
                         1) Only one person can participate in the event.
                         2) The participants are not allowed to bring any additional material.
                         3) Persons involved in any kind of malpractice will be immediately disqualified.
                         4) There are two rounds in Bug Challenge(Preliminary round & Final Round)

Preliminary round:

                 1) Moderate coding based questions will be asked.
                 2) Questions will be based on C,C++,Java & Python.
                 3) 20 questions should be answered in 20 minutes.
                 4) Top scorers will be qualified for next level.            

Final Round:

                 1) Debug the code of programming language C,C++,Java & Python.
                 2) Time limit will be 30 minutes.
                 3) One who solves errors first will be the winner.
                 4) Result of the round are not subject to discussion and judge’s decision is final.
